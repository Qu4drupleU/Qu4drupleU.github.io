01
Volatility Risk Premium Strategy

Quantitative research into whether trading the spread between implied volatility (VIX) and realized volatility generates consistent risk-adjusted returns. Fits GARCH(1,1) to SPY returns, constructs a vol-targeted long/short signal with 5bps transaction costs, and stress-tests across regimes (GFC, COVID, rate hike cycle). Includes out-of-sample validation (2005–2014 train, 2015–2024 test), sensitivity analysis over VRP thresholds, and a critical examination of post-publication crowding and Volmageddon 2018.

02
Pairs Trading — Statistical Arbitrage on CRSP Universe

Cointegration-based pairs trading strategy applied to the full CRSP equity universe (1925–2022). Filters the top 5,000 securities by average daily volume, identifies highly correlated pairs (ρ > 0.9) within SIC industry groups, and trades mean-reversion of the price spread using 20-day rolling Bollinger bands. Benchmarked against the S&P 500 (2010–2020) with transaction costs and slippage baked in. A second hypothesis tests whether strategy profitability varies systematically across industries using Sharpe ratio decomposition.

Real-time ADS-B Tracking & Collision Warning System

Live aircraft surveillance platform with 3D model visualization, flight path prediction cones, and per-type filtering (Large Jets, Small Jets, Light Aircraft, Rotary Wing, Military). Collision detection uses CPA (Closest Point of Approach) analysis with a risk-scoring system and color-coded warnings. Anomaly detection layer combines heuristic spoofing detection and ML-based models to flag unrealistic vertical rates, implausible ground speeds, abrupt heading changes, and position jumps in real time.
